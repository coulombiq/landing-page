Charge time and degredation of battery life are major obstacles to widespread electrification.
Coulombiq's technology enables fast charging to 100% without the capacity loss that typically results from fast charging.

Why wait?

Today's superchargers limit high rate charging to ~70% capacity to preserve battery life. Full charges take about 75 minutes. Grounded in electrochemical insights, Coulombiq enables 0-100% charging of Li-ion batteries in 30 minutes while preserving capacity for substantially more cycles.
